In the summer of 2022, I worked as a factory worker at Del Ponti, an injection molding company. This experience provided me with a deep understanding of the manufacturing industry. I was involved in the production process, assisting with the injection molding operations and ensuring quality control of the manufactured products. This role demanded attention to detail, precision, and adherence to safety protocols. Collaborating with a team of professionals allowed me to enhance my teamwork skills and adapt to the demands of a dynamic work environment.
